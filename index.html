About Stopawatch

This stopwatch is a simple stopwatch created and maintained by Oyintare Paul, a MERN stack learning developer as a little project to test and improve his skills. This is just a minimal version as he is still bringing in new features to make this stopwatch more useful and beautiful.

Check back later for additional features.

How to install

  • Open the menu on your browser by clicking the three dots or similar icon.
  • Click on Add to home screen and follow the instructions for your browser.
